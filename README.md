##  What is Waddle?

Waddle is a **fitness app that turns your real-world walks and runs into a live strategy game**.

You step outside, start moving, and trace paths through your neighborhood. When you close a loop, that area becomes **your territory** ‚ÄĒ your very own kingdom on the map. The more you move, the bigger your kingdom grows. Stop moving, and your rivals can come and take it all away.

> *It's not just about counting steps. It's about* **owning your city.**

<br/>

##  The Idea Behind It

Most fitness apps track what you do and reward you with numbers ‚ÄĒ calories, steps, streaks. Waddle rewards you with something more tangible: **land**.

We wanted to answer a simple question:

> *What if going for a walk felt like playing a game?*

That question became Waddle. Every route you run becomes a border. Every closed loop becomes a claimed zone. Every day you skip, your kingdom weakens. The city is always in play ‚ÄĒ and so are you.

<br/>

##  What Can You Do?

<div align="center">

| | Feature | Description |
|---|---|---|
|  | **Claim Territory** | Trace a closed route on a real map while walking or running. That enclosed area becomes yours. |
|  | **Grow Your Kingdom** | The more ground you cover, the larger your domain on the leaderboard. |
|  | **Invade Others** | Run through a rival's territory to challenge and conquer it. |
|  | **Defend What's Yours** | Stay active every few days or risk losing your land to more dedicated rivals. |
|  | **Leaderboard** | See how your kingdom stacks up against everyone else in real time. |
|  | **Track Your Health** | Log nutrition, monitor protein & carb intake, and get AI-powered diet insights. |
|  | **Earn Rewards** | Keep moving to unlock items in the in-app shop and power up your profile. |
